HISTORY: 

	Pong is one of the earliest arcade video games and the very first sports arcade video game.	
	It is a table tennis sports game featuring simple two-dimensional graphics.
	Pong quickly became a success and is the first commercially successful arcade video game machine, 
	which helped to establish the video game industry.
	
	Developer(s) 	Atari
	Designer(s) 	Allan Alcorn
	Platform(s) 	Arcade
	Release date(s) 29 November 1972


FUNCTION KEYS:

	For Player 1:
	- Left Button for movement of the racquet to the left
	- Right Button for movement of the racquet to the right
	- Up Button for smash

	For Player 2:
	- A Button for movement of the racquet to the left
	- D Button for the movement of the racquet to the right
	- W Button for smash

RULES:

	1. The Game is a 2-player game
	2. The first to reach 3 points wins the game
	3. Every player can smash if the smash button is clicked when the ball collides with the racquet

HOW THE PROGRAM WORKS:

	1. The speed increases by 1 as the ball collides with a racquet
	2. When the smash button is clicked when the ball collides with the racquet, the speed increases by 5
